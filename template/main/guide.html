üìã Additional Notes

5Writing Helpful Additional Notes

Use this section to provide important information that doesn't fit in other fields. This could include dress code, parking instructions, special requests, or anything else your guests should know.

What to Include in Additional Notes:
  • Dress Code: "Casual attire", "Black tie optional", "Beach formal"
  • Parking Information: "Free parking in the west lot", "Street parking available"
  • Special Instructions: "Please bring a swimsuit", "Gifts are not necessary"
  • COVID Guidelines: "Masks optional", "Vaccination required"
  • Accessibility: "Wheelchair accessible entrance on the north side"
