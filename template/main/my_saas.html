SaaS E-Invitation Platform – Full Overview

1. Concept Summary

Your SaaS is a digital platform that replaces traditional paper invitations and expensive event services. It allows users to:
  • Create professional e-invitations in minutes.
  • Manage guests easily through a dashboard.
  • Send personalized invitation links in bulk via WhatsApp and Email.
  • Track RSVPs and number of attendees in real time.
  • Use a Shared Gallery feature to let guests upload event photos (reduces or replaces cameraman cost).

2. Main Features

A. Event Type Selection

Users choose their event category:
  • Wedding
  • Birthday
  • Baptism
  • First Communion
  • Aaked Karan / Engagement
  • Custom events
Each category includes multiple professionally designed themes.

B. Guest Management System

  • Import guests via Excel or Access file.
  • Manual guest entry form.
  • Bulk add feature.
  • Each guest receives a unique link with their name embedded.
  • Status tracking: RSVP, number of additional guests, decline.
  • Dashboard with statistics: total invites, accepted, pending, final headcount.

C. Automated Bulk Sending

  • 1-click sending system via WhatsApp API or email.
  • Each guest receives their personalized link.
  • No manual messaging needed.

D. Shared Gallery Feature

  • Host generates a QR code for the event.
  • Guests scan it and directly upload photos to the shared gallery.
  • Instant community album replaces or reduces the need for a cameraman.

3. Features to Add (Recommended)

  • AI Invitation Builder: user enters event details → AI generates full invitation design.
  • Automated Reminder System: WhatsApp/email reminders 24h or 3 days before event.
  • Seat/Table Assignments: auto-generate seating map.
  • Check-in System: host scans guest QR on arrival.
  • Premium Themes Marketplace: designers upload templates, you take commissions.
  • Event Budget Planner: user tracks all event costs inside your platform.
  • Digital Gift Registry or Money Envelope: optional add-on.

4. Marketing Strategy – Lebanon

Target Groups

  • Wedding planners
  • Event organizers
  • Flower shops
  • Printing shops (offer partnership instead of competition)
  • Churches and wedding venues
  • Universities (graduation parties)

Lebanon-Specific Strategies

  • WhatsApp-first marketing: most Lebanese rely on WhatsApp → show how your bulk-send feature saves days of work.
  • Partnership with venues: give them a free dashboard; they promote you to every couple.
  • Arabic + English templates: huge advantage.
  • Cheap trial plan: e.g., 5$ for first event.
  • Instagram reels: invite creation in 20 seconds → viral.

5. Marketing Strategy – Global

  • SEO targeting keywords: “digital wedding invitation”, “RSVP app”, “event invitation creator”.
  • Launch on Product Hunt.
  • Partner with wedding blogs + influencers.
  • Offer white-label solutions for event companies.
  • Create templates for specific cultures: Indian weddings, Western weddings, Latino events, etc.
  • Offer multi-language invitations automatically.

6. Monetization

  • Pay per event: $5–$20
  • Monthly plans for event organizers.
  • Premium templates marketplace.
  • Add-ons: reminder system, custom domain, analytics pack.

7. Final Notes

Your platform solves three real problems:
  • Time wasted writing & delivering invitations.
  • Lack of tracking for RSVPs.
  • High photography cost for events.
With proper marketing, this SaaS can scale in Lebanon and globally.
